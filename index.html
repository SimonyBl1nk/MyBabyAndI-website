Our Mission

Built By Women, For Women

We exist to break down the barriers that women from ethnically diverse backgrounds face — isolation, language, lack of networks, and limited access to opportunity.

Through peer support, mentoring, employability workshops, and community events, we help new and expectant mothers find their footing, their voice, and their future in Scotland.

What We Do

Three Pillars of Support

🤝

Peer Support

Safe, welcoming spaces where mothers connect, share experiences, and support one another through the joys and challenges of motherhood and life in Scotland.

💼

Employment Access

CV workshops, interview coaching, and direct links to employers — helping women re-enter the workforce with confidence and skills that are recognised.

🌱

Community Connection

Events, partnerships and cultural celebrations that reduce isolation and build lasting friendships within Glasgow and Renfrewshire.
